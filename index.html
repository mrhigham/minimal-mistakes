“How we do things on screen”

Our Digital Language System (or DLS) allows us to create and maintain a shared language of principles and patterns to inform the design and development of our digital properties. It is used to help create consistent user experiences for our audience inside and outside the walls of our museum.

The DLS will adapt over time.
